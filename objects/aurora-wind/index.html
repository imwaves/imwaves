Aurora Wind 31 Dec 2022

audiovisual

The ever-rushing stream of fluctuations refracts against matter, reluctantly gives it shape and provokes more and more new outbreaks of discoveries. Just like the solar wind, penetrating through the earth’s magnetosphere, it modifies the DNA of all livings, and with all its greatness it dominates the chance.
Inspired by nothern lights (aurora borealis) on a recent trip to Iceland.
