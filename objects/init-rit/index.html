Initiation Ritual @ Wild Jam 50 15 Oct 2022

gamedev audiovisual

Extremely spontaneous decision to assemble a team just for 1 week to learn Godot engine and to get the playable game.
I set a goal to achieve visual specific atmoshpere of ancientry with concave projecting rock paintings to the relief stone wall; immersive natural camera motion like somebody really sitting in front of the wall; distinct moon and sun light; fog, smoke, crickets particle systems. And ofcause audio effects different in atmosphere and mood in scenes.

2nd and 4th place in Graphics and Audio charts, 9th overall.
